DadBuds terms

Terms of use

DadBuds is a Spokane beta for measuring interest in informal local plans for dads. Submitting the form does not guarantee access, invitations, events, or any specific feature.

DadBuds is not childcare

DadBuds helps adults coordinate informal local plans. It does not provide childcare, safety screening, supervision, transportation, emergency services, or professional advice.

Plans can change

Any future DadBuds plans are informal and may be changed, canceled, or paused while the pilot is being evaluated.

Consent-first communication

Marketing consent is optional. If SMS or email updates are used, they should stay limited to DadBuds beta plans and product updates, and you should be able to opt out.

Commpanny infrastructure

Commpanny may operate shared infrastructure for landing pages, analytics, and lead management while preserving DadBuds-specific consent and audience boundaries.
