DadBuds privacy

Privacy notice

DadBuds is a small Spokane beta. We collect only the information needed to understand local interest, coordinate follow-up, and decide whether the pilot deserves more work.

What we collect

We collect your submitted email, ZIP code, Spokane beta interest, ZIP-code launch interest, referral code, consent choices, and campaign attribution.

How we use it

We use this information to evaluate Spokane-area demand, understand where DadBuds should launch next, follow up about DadBuds beta plans if you consent, and improve the pilot.

Commpanny parent role

Commpanny may operate shared landing-page and marketing infrastructure for DadBuds. DadBuds consent is venture-specific. Joining DadBuds does not automatically subscribe you to unrelated Commpanny brands.

Removal requests

Reply to a DadBuds follow-up email or contact the operator directly to request removal from the pilot list.
